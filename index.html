BRAINSTORMING

This is our brainstorming idea! We used Mind Map to help with our brainstorming

Problem: Sitio Bebe is far from a natural waterway so the local government often imports clean water for the residents, or opens wells. Because of this, there is often a drought in the area especially in summer. The area is experiencing a shortage of health professionals, so residents rarely have anyone close to them whenever someone gets sick.

Objectives: Donate money to help mitigate/solve the current crisis in Sitio Bebe

Strategy: Since we are an event organizer we could start a concert so that we could earn enough money not just to earn but to also help SItio Bebe. We could use YouTube and Create a Webpage to be able to advertise our concert. Our first chosen genre is gaming so that our videos can be noticed by people who are interested in gaming. And also create a website to help with the advertising.

Revision: We think our plan might fail since there are already many gaming YouTubers that would make our videos not be noticed at all. We changed our genre to an Animated awareness video so that we could not just advertise our concert but still let the viewers know why we were promoting the concert. Also, most people enjoy watching animated videos.

Solution: We will make a concert and use our knowledge of creating YouTube videos, animations, and websites to help advertise and gain viewers for someone tp purchase our tickets for the concert.

Youtube Content

We decided to choose Animated Awareness Videos, because we discovered that viewers prefer animation over videos, and this preference increases if the movies contain difficult information and facts. And also because animated videos are more interesting than other forms of videos. This is primarily due to the fact that watching a corporate video is made exciting and enjoyable by animation. Animation also improves audience focus and engagement with the core topic. (ImpressVideo, 2018)

Target Audience Of Our Youtube

Ages 25-34 are our target audiences because based on NextGrowthLabs (2023)(A mobile growth company) there are 522.5 million users, followed by 35-44 year olds with 422 Million users, 18-24 year olds with 379.7 million users, 45-54 with 303 million users and 55-64 year olds with 222.2 million users. “While 56% of the users are male and 44% are females” (TheSocialSheperd, 2023)
